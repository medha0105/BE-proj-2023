The Punjab State Cooperative Bank Ltd. was established at Shimla in the year 1949 as
Apex Bank of three tier short term Cooperative Credit structure. There are 20 District
Central Co-operative Banks having 820 branches in the State of Punjab and in the area
of Chandigarh affiliated to it. The Bank was established to help to provide timely and
adequate flow of credit to the farmers for agriculture and allied activities through PACS.
The Bank has successfully completed more than Seven decades of service to the State
of Punjab in general and to the rural and farming community in particular. The Bank and
its affiliated DCCBs are fully automated with CBS (Finacle) running in all its branches as
a service provided by M/s Wipro Ltd.
