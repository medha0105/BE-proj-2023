The Punjab State Co-operative bank intends to purchase around 2000 Micro ATMs in
the first phase of the Project, which may increase to around 5 to 6 thousands in the
coming two to three years.  The objective to invite the EOI is to provide the demonstration of
Micro ATM  hardware along with understanding of software and various interface/
gateway functionality  . The micro ATM devices should comply with IBA, IDRBT, NPCI and
UIDAI and  formulated micro ATM 1.5.1 standards.
